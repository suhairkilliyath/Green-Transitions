Spatial Distribution of India's GDP

This 3D tool shows how Indiaâ€™s GDP (2015-2016 at constant 2011-2012 prices) is spatially distributed across the country. Each block on the map is a 1km2 gridded cell of GDP output (see legend for values based on colour). This tool builds on our earlier work that showed the spatial distribution of 2006 GDP.


In this version, we have introduced two new features:


  • A Compare States tool which allows the user to view the spatial distribution of economic activity of two states side-by-side.

This tool is a work-in-progress. We are working to add more data and layers that we hope will offer more insights, and welcome any feedback and queries at shreyas.thottuvai@idfcinstitute.org
